Built for servers that
actually care about who joins.

Most Minecraft servers rely on a username and a handshake. That's it. MineAuth goes further — it creates a verified, session-based link between a player's in-game identity and a confirmed account, so server owners always know exactly who's on their whitelist.

Whether you're running a tight-knit SMP with friends or managing a community server, MineAuth gives you a simple, professional verification flow. Players join a lightweight verification server, run one command, and they're linked. No plugins. No databases to manage. No complexity.

For players who can't connect in-game, there's a manual email fallback — handled by your team through Discord, keeping the process human and in your control.

// how_it_works

Simple by design.

01
Player gets an invite
A server owner sends a MineAuth invite link. The player opens it and sees a clean, branded verification page specific to that server.
02
They join the verify server
The player connects to verify.mineauth.net and runs a unique one-time command in chat to confirm their identity.
03
Account is linked
MineAuth confirms the session, links the player's Minecraft account, and your team gets notified. The player is ready to join the real server.
04
Email fallback if needed
Can't get in-game? Players can verify via the email on their Microsoft account instead. Your team handles the code manually through Discord.
// why_mineauth

Everything you need.
Nothing you don't.

No plugins required
MineAuth works entirely at the network layer. Your main server doesn't need any modifications, mods, or plugin installs.
Session-based tokens
Every verification uses a unique, one-time token. Tokens expire when used, so codes can never be reused or shared to gain access.
Email fallback
Players who can't connect in-game can verify using the email tied to their Microsoft account. Fully manual — your team stays in control.
Discord integration
Verification requests and code submissions are delivered straight to your Discord via webhooks — no separate dashboard needed.
Fast verification
In-game verification takes under 30 seconds. The entire flow — from invite link to verified account — is designed to get out of the way fast.
Branded per-server
Each invite page is customized with your server name. Players see your server's identity, not a generic flow — keeping the experience yours.
